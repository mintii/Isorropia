Kingdom of Langthold
850k sq miles

Culture

Hair is worn generally just past the shoulder and braided into one long back braid or sometimes a longer back braid and side braids Formal dress in the summer consists of tunics with flared sleeves and a baggy trouser tucked into mid-shin high boots. The outfit is supplemented with a wool undershirt and cloak worn over one shoulder during the winter months

Army

The Langtvorn: Essentially a mercenary company, in order to The Levies: Each landholder is required to supply their liege with a certain number of troops in times of war. They tend to be trained and equipped with their own supplies however and are rarely top notch

The Highlands

5 million Langts live in the highlands in much the same lifestyle as they have always lived. They are hunters and gatherers, some sending furs and meat down the river for gold.
